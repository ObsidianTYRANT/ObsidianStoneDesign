Who Am I?

My name is Justin Michael Rocha. I am a 25 year old graphic design student currently attending Fresno State University in Fresno, California. Web design is my area of focus at this time, though I am interested in illustration as well. Gaming is my other great passion and I'd love to break into the gaming industry one day. That is the ultimate goal for my career. That slice of the entertainment industry is a rapidly growing field that needs people like me that have the drive and passion to do good work for good games.

The services that I offer range anywhere from birthday cards and invitations to brand identity and web design.
