Who Am I?

My name is Justin Michael Rocha. I am a graphic design student currently attending Fresno State University in Fresno, California. Web design is my area of focus at this time, although I am very interested in illustration as well. Art, movies and video games are some of my greatest passions. Movies and games have always captivated and inspired me. Some day I hope to break into the entertainment industry and unleash my vast imagination on the world.
